It is vital we take care of our ecosystem

Ocean acidification is one repercussion of climate change and occurs due to a reduction in pH which develops as a result of the loss of Hydrogen ions from human carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions, for example, through deforestation and causes a reaction of carbonate chemistry (Doney et al., 2009). There is a wide range of data in this topic area which explains the many cycles of biogeochemical compounds that occurs within the seas around the world (Doney et al., 2009).
